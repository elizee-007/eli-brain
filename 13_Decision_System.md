# Purpose

The Decision System provides a structured framework for making, recording, evaluating, and reviewing decisions across the ELIZEE ecosystem. It ensures that important decisions are based on logic, evidence, long-term thinking, and documented reasoning.

---

# Objectives

- Improve decision quality.
- Reduce impulsive decisions.
- Document important choices.
- Learn from previous decisions.
- Build consistent decision-making habits.

---

# Decision Categories

## Personal Decisions

Life, habits, health, and personal growth.

---

## Career Decisions

Jobs, business opportunities, certifications, and professional development.

---

## Project Decisions

Planning, architecture, features, priorities, and project management.

---

## Financial Decisions

Budgeting, investments, purchases, and savings.

---

## Learning Decisions

Courses, books, research topics, and skill development.

---

# Decision Framework

Every important decision should include:

- Decision Title
- Purpose
- Context
- Available Options
- Benefits
- Risks
- Estimated Impact
- Final Decision
- Reasoning
- Review Date
- Lessons Learned

---

# Decision Lifecycle

1. Identify the Problem
2. Collect Information
3. Generate Alternatives
4. Evaluate Risks & Benefits
5. Make the Decision
6. Execute
7. Review Results
8. Document Lessons

---

# Decision Principles

- Think long-term.
- Use evidence whenever possible.
- Consider multiple options.
- Avoid emotional decisions.
- Learn from mistakes.
- Document important outcomes.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Decision System will become the intelligence layer of ELIZEE, helping preserve reasoning behind important choices and continuously improving future decision-making.
