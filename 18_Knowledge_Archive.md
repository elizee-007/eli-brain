# Purpose

The Knowledge Archive is the permanent storage system for verified knowledge collected throughout the ELIZEE ecosystem. It preserves valuable information for future reference, learning, and decision-making.

---

# Objectives

- Preserve important knowledge.
- Organize archived information.
- Enable quick retrieval.
- Prevent knowledge loss.
- Support long-term learning.

---

# Archive Categories

- Personal Knowledge
- AI Knowledge
- Technical Knowledge
- Islamic Knowledge
- Project Knowledge
- Research Notes
- Reference Materials

---

# Archive Information

Each archive entry should include:

- Title
- Category
- Summary
- Source
- Date Added
- Version
- Tags
- Notes

---

# Archive Rules

- Archive only verified information.
- Avoid duplicate entries.
- Update outdated knowledge.
- Keep information organized.
- Preserve version history.

---

# Archive Lifecycle

1. Collect
2. Verify
3. Organize
4. Archive
5. Review
6. Update

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Knowledge Archive will become the permanent digital memory of the ELIZEE ecosystem, preserving valuable knowledge for continuous growth and future generations.
