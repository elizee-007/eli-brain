# Purpose

The Research Archive is a centralized repository for collecting, organizing, verifying, and preserving research conducted throughout the ELIZEE ecosystem. It ensures that valuable findings remain accessible and reusable for future learning and project development.

---

# Objectives

- Preserve research permanently.
- Organize research systematically.
- Verify information before archiving.
- Support evidence-based decisions.
- Build a reliable knowledge foundation.

---

# Research Categories

- Artificial Intelligence
- Civil Engineering
- Technology
- Software Development
- Islamic Studies
- Science
- Business
- Personal Development

---

# Research Record

Each research entry should include:

- Title
- Category
- Objective
- Summary
- Sources
- Key Findings
- Conclusion
- Date
- Tags
- Notes

---

# Research Standards

- Use reliable sources.
- Verify important facts.
- Separate facts from opinions.
- Keep research organized.
- Update outdated information.

---

# Research Lifecycle

1. Identify Topic
2. Collect Information
3. Verify Sources
4. Analyze Findings
5. Document Results
6. Archive
7. Review

---

# Long-Term Vision

The Research Archive will become a trusted research library that supports innovation, learning, and informed decision-making across the ELIZEE ecosystem.
