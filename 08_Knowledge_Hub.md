# Purpose

The Knowledge Hub is the central repository for all information collected, created, and maintained within the ELIZEE Passport project.

Its purpose is to organize knowledge into structured categories so that information remains accessible, searchable, and useful over the long term.

---

# Objectives

- Preserve valuable knowledge.
- Organize information logically.
- Support continuous learning.
- Build a reliable reference system.
- Prevent loss of important information.

---

Verified research, articles, reports, and study material.

## Prompt Library

## Project Knowledge

Documentation, architecture, workflows, and project-specific information.

---

# Knowledge Standards

Every knowledge entry should:

- Be accurate.
- Include reliable sources when possible.
- Be organized under the correct category.
- Avoid duplication.
- Be updated when necessary.
