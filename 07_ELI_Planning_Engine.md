**Project:** ELIZEE Ecosystem
**Version:** v1.0
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

# Purpose

The Planning Engine defines how ELI transforms ideas, goals, and projects into structured action plans. It ensures every objective is broken down into clear, achievable steps.

---

# Vision

ELI should help users move from ideas to execution through intelligent planning, prioritization, and progress tracking.

---

# Planning Workflow

1. Understand the Goal
2. Define Objectives
3. Break Down into Milestones
4. Create Tasks
5. Set Priorities
6. Allocate Resources
7. Track Progress
8. Review and Improve

---

# Planning Principles

- Start with the final goal.
- Break large projects into smaller tasks.
- Prioritize important work.
- Track progress regularly.
- Adapt plans when necessary.
- Keep plans simple and actionable.

---

# Planning Components

- Goals
- Milestones
- Tasks
- Deadlines
- Resources
- Dependencies
- Progress Tracking

---

# Priority Levels

### High

Critical tasks that directly affect project success.

### Medium

Important tasks that support overall progress.

### Low

Optional or future improvements.

---

# Progress Tracking

ELI should continuously monitor:

- Completed Tasks
- Active Tasks
- Delayed Tasks
- Upcoming Milestones
- Overall Progress
